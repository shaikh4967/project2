Our Milestone’s

With moderate beginning since 1983 we had a long journey of progress and growth in Diamond Business more than 3 decade. It's pleasure to share some of our glimse of acheivement with you.

1983

A Humble Beginnings ...

Foundation of diamond manufacturing business by Mr. Ranchhodbhai Detroja in 1983 with 20 workers at Navsari, Gujarat.

1986

Gujarat ...

Establishment of second manufacturing facility at Morbi, Gujarat.

1995

Change in structure of the Company...

Changed the constitution of the firm from proprietary to partnership. A third manufacturing facility for diamond polishing started at Unjha, Gujarat, India.

1997

Gujarat ...

1998

Achievement ...

We achieved the highest man power strengths in industry.

2002

Fifth manufacturing facility ...

Established fifth manufacturing facility at Dhrol, Gujarat, India.

2003

Export House ...

In 2003, Ratnakala was recognized as an “Export House” by the Director General of Foreign Trade, The Government of India.

2004-05

Our Achievement ...

Achieved total revenues of US$ 25 million in a financial year for the first time

2005

Green Energy Initiative ...

Initiative in Green Energy. We've installed two wind turbines generators having capacity of 2.5 MW in Maharashtra, India

2006-07

Financial Achievement ...

Achieved total revenues of US$ 50 million.

2007

Major Rough Buyer ...

Recognized as a major rough buyer with M/s Alrosa Ltd, Moscow, Russia.

2008

Captive Wind Turbine ...

We have installed captive wind turbine generators at Gujarat, India in 2008. Since then, we use the clean energy in polishing the diamonds in all the factories of Ratnakala.

2010

Registered with UNFCCC ...

Ratnakala registered with UNFCCC for CDM benefits for carbon credit.

2014

Improved Term Rating ...

CRISIL improved Ratnakala’s Long - Term rating from BBB to BBB+.

2015

Diamond polishing Unit at Surat ...

Started diamond polishing Unit at Surat, India for dossier and up to 2 carat.
